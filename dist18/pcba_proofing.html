PCBA proofing is a trial production to determine the feasibility of mass production. PCBA proofing refers to PCBA processing manufacturers purchasing a small amount of trial production by purchasing relevant electronic components through electronic product development solutions designed for customers or PCB electronic document files and bills of materials provided by customers. However, the unit price of PCBA proofing is relatively expensive compared to the unit price of mass production.

PCBA proofing process

Customers only need to provide the results they want and the products they need to achieve. The product PCBA design, material procurement, proofing production is handed over to GOLONCT, and professional PCBA processing manufacturers provide PCBA proofing one-stop service.

PCBA proofing process

  1. Product development and design

    The first step of PCBA proofing is to design and develop the corresponding electronic products according to customer requirements. If the customer has already developed the products, the following process will be directly carried out.

  2. Material procurement

    According to the BOM bill of materials of the existing electronic product development program, the corresponding electronic components are purchased, tested and debugged as needed.

  3. Proofing production

    After all the preparations are ready, the PCB boards and components are delivered to the SMT and DIP production lines for placement and soldering. The process will have strict furnace temperature control to ensure production quality.

  4. Test delivery

    According to the PCBA test plan provided by the customer, strict PCBA test, such as ICT and FCT test, is carried out to ensure smooth wiring and full solder joints, so that the PCB board can be delivered to the customer normally. After the PCB board is finished, it is protected and packaged to avoid damage. If the customer receives any problems after receiving the proof plate, they can request rework for repair.
