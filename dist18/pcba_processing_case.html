Wireless signal amplifier design

                    The wireless signal amplifier is a relay transmission device for wireless signals. It uses intelligent single-chip technology to automatically learn the address code

 

and data code of the PT2262 series (315MHZ or 433MHZ) remote control signals, and increase the power forwarding to increase the transmission distance. The function.

 

                    When remote control equipment or wireless anti-theft alarm system is used in factories, workshops, buildings and villas, the remote control distance is too short due

 

to environmental influence or the remote control is unreliable. The smart wireless signal amplifier can solve this problem well.

 

                    The intelligent wireless signal amplifier uses the DC 12V working voltage polarity inside and outside the negative voltage. After the 12V power supply is turned on,

 

the green LED lights up. After receiving the required remote control signal, the high power is forwarded immediately, and the indicator light turns orange. The time will vary

 

depending on the oscillating resistance, which is usually 3 to 6 seconds.
