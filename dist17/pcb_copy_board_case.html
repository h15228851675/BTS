Localization example of DCU module for subway traction inverter

The most powerful countries in the field of core control components for metro vehicles are Germany, France, Canada, Japan, etc. The three multinational companies such as Bombardier, Siemens and Alstom account for more than 50% of the global market. The domestic CRRC Group and other units authorized the production of core control components of foreign companies in the form of joint ventures, but the core technology is still not in control.

Traction inverter is one of them. GOLONCT relies on its own strong R&D capability to localize its core component traction inverter DCU module. Its basic functions are as follows:





Each vehicle of a certain subway company is equipped with a traction inverter for each vehicle, which supplies power to four parallel AC asynchronous traction motors. Each traction inverter is controlled by a 32-bit microcontroller (MCU), using IGBT components and modular design, PWM drive, monitoring and protection of the temperature of the brake chopper IGBT, and traction inversion based on the feedback signal Thermal stress, traction and electric braking force calculation, PID optimal closed- loop control to ensure the optimal use efficiency of the motor. Our company undertakes the repair of this board and the development of domestically produced independent intellectual property spare parts products. At present, the DCU board parts produced in China have passed the test of the ground platform of a subway company, and the main line sports car test is being carried out.
